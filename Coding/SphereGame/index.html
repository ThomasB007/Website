(This game is requires a Keyboard and Mouse)

Controls:

Move Forward --- "W" or "Up Arrow"
Move Sideways --- "A, D" or "Left Arrow, Right Arrow"
Jump --- Space Bar

Aim of The Game:

Either play Endless mode, in which you have to get the furthest as possible, or play in Normal mode, where you have to complete levels and get to the end the quickest. Enjoy!
